About Us

12+ Years of Excellence in Tile and Sanitary Solutions

Our Story

Building Trust Since 2012

Arshad Tiles & Sanitary was established in 2012 with a simple mission: to provide the people of Shabqadar and surrounding areas with access to high-quality tiles, sanitary ware, and building materials at affordable prices.

What started as a small shop in Main Shabqadar Bazar has grown into one of the most trusted names in the region. Over the past 12 years, we have served thousands of satisfied customers, from homeowners undertaking small renovations to contractors managing large commercial projects.

Our success is built on three pillars: quality products, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service. We believe that everyone deserves access to beautiful, durable materials for their homes and businesses, and we work hard to make that a reality.

Today, we stock over 1000 products from leading national and international brands, ensuring that our customers have access to the latest designs and technologies in the industry.
